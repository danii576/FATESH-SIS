About Fatesh Sis

Welcome to Fatesh SIS, established on November 2, 2023, a beauty salon located in Barreiro, Portugal, dedicated to helping you look and feel your best. We offer a full range of services including nails, manicure, pedicure, waxing, threading, eyebrow and hair coloring, along with head and shoulder massages. Whether you want a refreshing hair wash, sleek straight hair, or gorgeous curls, our skilled team is here to pamper you from head to toe in a warm and welcoming environment.

Fatesh is a welcoming, family-owned beauty salon in Barreiro, Portugal. We offer premium, luxurious services to help you look and feel your best.
